Why Your Support Matters

At Shree Saraswati Shishu Mandir, we believe that quality education should be accessible to all deserving students. Your generous donations help us maintain and improve our educational facilities, provide scholarships to underprivileged students, and enhance the overall learning experience.

As a non-profit educational institution, we rely on the support of our community, alumni, and well-wishers to continue our mission of providing holistic education that nurtures both academic excellence and moral values.

Tax Benefits

Income Tax Relief Under Section 80G

All donations made to Shree Saraswati Shishu Mandir are eligible for income tax relief under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Donors can claim a deduction of 50% of the donation amount from their taxable income.

Upon receiving your donation, we will issue an official receipt that can be used for claiming tax benefits. Please ensure you provide your complete details including PAN number for tax purposes.

Note: For donations exceeding ₹2,000, payment through cash is not eligible for tax benefits as per Income Tax rules. Please use bank transfer, check, or online payment methods.
