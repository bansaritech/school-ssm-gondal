Tax Benefits

Income Tax Benefits Under Section 80G

All donations to Shree Saraswati Shishu Mandir are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Our institution has been granted approval by the Income Tax Department, making your contributions eligible for tax deductions.

50% Deduction

Get 50% tax deduction on the donated amount from your taxable income

Official Receipt

Receive an official 80G receipt for tax filing purposes

Any Amount

Donations of any amount are eligible for tax benefits

How to Claim Tax Benefits:

  1. Make your donation to Shree Saraswati Shishu Mandir
  2. Provide your PAN details and complete address
  3. Receive the 80G certificate from us
  4. Submit the certificate while filing your income tax return
  5. Claim the deduction under Section 80G

Please provide your PAN details and complete address to receive the tax exemption certificate. For any queries related to tax benefits, please contact our office.
